Our history

Wooden products have been known in Ukraine since ancient times, especially in areas where wood was the main material. Utensils, tools, boats, household utensils, etc. were made of wood. The oldest methods of wood processing that have survived to the present day are burning and extraction.

Wood has long been a favourite and valuable material. It is strong and durable and has a low specific weight. Stains, oils and varnishes reliably protect wood products from water and, at the same time, improve the quality of the wood.

In-depth knowledge of the properties of wood and other materials has always been the unchanging basis of folk art.

Our production workshops are equipped with all the necessary modern and reliable equipment, which enables us to provide a wide range of services and manufacture a wide variety of products. In our production we use different types of wood, carefully select and check the raw materials and then carry out all the necessary work stages. Being a manufacturer, our prices compare favourably with those of other companies offering natural wood furniture. Wood products are one of our core businesses, so we guarantee high quality of the final product.
